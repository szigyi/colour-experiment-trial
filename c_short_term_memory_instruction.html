Short Term Memory Instructions

This cognitive task consists of 30 trials, 5 of which are practice trials, they will not be included in data collection.

On the next screen, you will be presented with a stimuli image for 250ms.

The image will then disappear.

It will be replaced by a screen containing an RGB (red, green, blue) colour slider.

Using the slider, please indicate along the scale, how much of each individual colour you saw in the previous stimuli image.

When you are ready, submit your answers to move on to the next trial.

The next trial will not begin until you do this.

Please click to begin.
