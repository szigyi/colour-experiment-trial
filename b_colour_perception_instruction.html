Colour Perception Instructions

This cognitive task consists of 30 trials, 5 of which are test trials, they will not be included in data collection.

On the next screen, you will be presented with a stimuli image. Underneath this, will be an RGB (red, green, blue) colour slider.

Using the slider, please indicate along the scale, how much of each individual colour you see in the stimuli image.

When you are ready, submit your answers to move on to the next trial.

The next trial will not begin until you do this.
